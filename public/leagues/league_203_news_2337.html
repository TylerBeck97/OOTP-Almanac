Texas on Top of American League West Division
Monday, September 29th , 2025
It wasn't easy, but the Texas Rangers are champions of the American League West Division.

The club faced challenges to the crown throughout the season, but in the end have edged their division rivals to grab the playoff spot.

"Relief. Happiness. Excitement," was how starting pitcher Jacob deGrom responded when asked what he's feeling after the clinch. "We're all looking forward to getting the playoffs started."

The team has a 94-68 record, which has wrapped up their 9th division title.

Team manager Bruce Bochy said it would be "extremely satisfying" to be the man at the helm when the team captures its 2nd World Series.
