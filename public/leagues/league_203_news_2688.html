San Diego, Los Angeles Make Trade
Saturday, November 29th , 2025
Earlier today, officials from the San Diego Padres and the Los Angeles Dodgers announced that they had finalized negotiations on a trade between the two teams. The deal will send 34-year old RHP Robert Suarez and $1,600,000 in cash to Los Angeles in exchange for 16-year old minor league RHP Roberto Cervantes.

San Diego will retain a portion of Suarez's remaining contract.

During his career, Suarez has appeared in 197 games and captured 38 saves. To date he has 18 wins, 9 losses, and a 2.81 ERA.
