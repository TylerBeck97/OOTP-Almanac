AL Announces Rookie of the Year Award
Monday, November 10th , 2025
The American League has presented its Rookie of the Year Award for 2025 and the honor went to Nick Kurtz, the fine Athletics first baseman.

The 22-year-old Athletics newcomer took the trophy by hitting .275 in 158 games and finished with 169 hits, 30 home runs, 112 RBIs and 82 runs scored.

He received 30 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Kyle Teel of the Chicago White Sox finished second in voting, while Max Muncy of the Athletics finished third.
