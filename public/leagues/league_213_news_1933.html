Carolina-Augusta Fight Results in Suspensions
Wednesday, August 27th , 2025
Christian Mitchelle of the Carolina Mudcats and Luke Sinnard of the Augusta GreenJackets will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Mudcats-GreenJackets game at Five County Stadium.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Mitchelle and Sinnard will begin serving them soon.

Thus far this year, Mitchelle has hit .333 with no home runs and 3 RBIs.

So far in 5 appearances this season, Sinnard has recorded 1 win and sustained 2 losses. He has pitched 27 innings, yielded 25 hits, fanned 41 and has a 5.67 ERA.
