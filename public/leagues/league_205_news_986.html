Ritter on a Rampage, Hits for Cycle
Saturday, June 28th , 2025
Ryan Ritter truly did it all at Rio Grande Credit Union Field at Isotopes Park.

The Albuquerque Isotopes shortstop hit for the cycle -- collecting 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run -- as he paced his club to victory, 12-2, over the Salt Lake Bees.

"You know that sweet sound you hear when you hit the ball just right? I heard that every time today," Ritter said later. "It was a heck of a feeling."

It was a game good enough for him to make a little history. He bombed Salt Lake pitching for 4 hits in 5 at-bats and his name will now go into the record books. Ritter also racked up 2 RBI and 1 run scored in the game.

Ryan Ritter hit an RBI triple in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Tommy Eveld in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

This season, Ritter is batting .336 with 27 RBIs, 26 runs scored and 7 home runs.
