Braves, Rockies Make Trade
Monday, November 24th , 2025
The Braves and Rockies announced a deal that was completed today. Atlanta sent 19-year old minor league RHP Rayven Antonio and 18-year old minor league RHP Yander Pinero to Colorado for 31-year old RHP Jose Ruiz. Both teams appear happy with the outcome of the trade. The general managers told the Baseball News Network they felt the swap was beneficial for all parties involved.


In his career, Ruiz has a 10-10 won-lost record with a 4.30 ERA. In 277 games he has recorded 1 save, and the 31-year-old has 281 strikeouts in 284.2 innings.
