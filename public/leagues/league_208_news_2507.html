Springfield Hurler Tops in Outstanding Pitcher Award Voting
Monday, October 20th , 2025
Darlin Saladin of the Springfield Cardinals is in the spotlight today. He was acclaimed the best pitcher in the Texas League and will be honored with the 2025 Outstanding Pitcher Award.

He started 19 games and compiled a 7-5 record with an ERA of 3.41 in 113.1 innings with 112 strikeouts.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 10. Jacob Steinmetz of the Amarillo Sod Poodles finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Eric Yost of the San Antonio Missions finished third.
