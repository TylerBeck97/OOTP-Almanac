League Suspends Bowser and Thompson for Brawl
Saturday, May 17th , 2025
Yesterday at Tempe Diablo Stadium Drew Bowser and Benny Thompson got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Chicago N (ACL) Cubs and Los Angeles A (ACL) Angels. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Bowser will have to sit out 3 while Thompson would be given a 7-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.
