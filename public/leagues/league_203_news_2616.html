Boston's Crochet Nets AL Cy Young Award
Wednesday, November 12th , 2025
The Boston Red Sox have a rising superstar or maybe even a budding Hall-of-Famer in the making. It's Garrett Crochet, this year's American League Cy Young Award selection. This blossoming hurler wowed fans, peers and sportswriters with a fabulous performance.

Crochet compiled a 15-8 record in 33 starts, tallied 223 strikeouts in 187.1 innings and recorded a 2.40 ERA. Other clubs managed only a .208 batting average against him.

He received 23 first place votes out of a possible 30. Max Fried of the New York Yankees finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Joe Ryan of the Minnesota Twins finished third.
