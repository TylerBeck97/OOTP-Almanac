Collins Belts 3 HR for Mets
Thursday, September 18th , 2025
Syracuse Mets first baseman Corey Collins sensed he was going to have a good day at the plate, but he still might have underestimated it a little.

Prior to the start of today's contest with the Charlotte Knights, Collins told teammates he was sure he would hit a home run. He did -- three times.

"During batting practice I really had my swing down pat," he recalled. "I felt some thunder in the lumber."

The big day from Collins pushed the Mets past the Knights, 16-3. To go along with the homers, he collected 8 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"I was seeing good, feeling good and swinging good," Collins told reporters after the game.

Corey Collins hit a solo-shot off Manuel Veloz in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a GRAND SLAM off Justin Dunn in the 4th, struck out in the 5th and hit a two-run home run off Jesse Scholtens in the 7th.

So far on the season, Collins is hitting .291 with 8 home runs, 24 RBIs and 16 runs scored. He has 23 hits and played in 26 games.
