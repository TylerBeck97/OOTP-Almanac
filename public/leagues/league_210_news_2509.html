Shaver Takes Top SAL Manager Honors
Monday, October 20th , 2025
Nate Shaver, skipper of the Asheville Tourists, was voted 2025 South Atlantic League Manager of the Year and accepted the award earlier today.

He guided Asheville to first place in the Southern Division with a 77-55 record.

"I'm very happy about this," he said at the press conference. "But I'm more happy about what my boys did on the field. They battled their butts off for me all year. They should get the credit."
