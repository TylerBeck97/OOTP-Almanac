Tampa Bay, Kansas City Confirm Deal
Tuesday, July 29th , 2025
You can't always get what you want, but sometimes you find a deal anyway. The Baseball News Network is reporting that Tampa Bay and Kansas City have made a swap with 24-year old minor league LF Brock Jones being shipped to the Royals and in return the Rays would get 35-year old RHP Seth Lugo.

Tampa Bay general manager Erik Neander said the two sides were close to a blockbuster trade before agreeing upon a smaller scale transaction.

"This is just a bit of housekeeping compared to what we wanted to do," he said. "I won't name names, but the potential deal we were close to making would have certainly grabbed some headlines."

A source inside the Tampa Bay organization said the two sides might not be done dealing, and another transaction could be in the works.


This season, Lugo has a 2-8 record with a 4.91 ERA in 13 starts.
