Guardians Win Arizona Complex League Central Crown
Friday, July 4th , 2025
The pennant has been won, the Cleveland (ACL) Guardians are champions of the Arizona Complex League Central.

Reliever Daiher Hernandez said his team is a "gritty and determined" club that were poised to win the division from day one.

"We knew the day the season opened that we'd be champions," he said. "It was just a matter of going out there and doing it."

At the helm, team manager Yan Rivera said his club is certainly not short on confidence.

"There's a lot of belief in the dugout and in the clubhouse," he said.

Cleveland (ACL)'s record currently sits at 32-20 and it's the 1st division win for the franchise.
