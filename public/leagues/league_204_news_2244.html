League Suspends Mauricio and Schultz for Brawl
Saturday, September 20th , 2025
If there wasn't a history between Ronny Mauricio of Syracuse and Noah Schultz of Charlotte, then there is one now. The incident between the two sparked a bench-clearing scrum with those two being the center of attention. Now they will miss 4 and 6 games respectively as a result of their actions. The league office announced their suspensions today.

"We're both really competitive," Mauricio said later, "and sometimes things go a little too far because of it."

This year, Mauricio has 27 home runs and a .338 batting average.

Schultz has posted a 6-9 record for the year with a 5.83 ERA. Teams are hitting .275 against him and he has rung up 156 strikeouts in 139 innings.
