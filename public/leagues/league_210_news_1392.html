Villarroel, Rojas Suspended for Fracas
Tuesday, July 22nd , 2025
Jancel Villarroel of the Asheville Tourists and Juan Rojas of the Aberdeen Ironbirds will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 3 and 7 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Tourists-Ironbirds game at McCormick Field.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Villarroel and Rojas will begin serving them soon.

On the year, Villarroel has hit .356 with 2 home runs, 12 RBIs and scored 23 runs.

This season in 10 starts, Juan Rojas has a record of 2-6 with 56 strikeouts in 50 innings and a 6.30 ERA.
