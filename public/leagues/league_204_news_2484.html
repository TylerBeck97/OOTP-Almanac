International League Announces Best Manager
Friday, October 17th , 2025
The Worcester Red Sox called a press conference to announce that field manager Chad S. Tracy had been named International League Manager of the Year earlier in the day. Staffers congratulated the 40-year-old baseball guru as he approached the podium.

"This city has allowed me to craft a winning ball club," said Tracy. "When you take on a new job, there's always some adjustment period, but everyone here has been cooperative. From the bat boys on up. Here's to many more successful seasons for the Worcester Red Sox!"

Tracy led Worcester to a 97-53 record this year and a first place finish in the East Division.
