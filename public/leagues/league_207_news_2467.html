Blue Wahoos Win Round 2
Monday, October 13th , 2025
The Pensacola Blue Wahoos earned their 1st Round 2 title and now have all winter to spend basking in the glory, while the beaten Knoxville Smokies will have plenty of time to wonder what went wrong.

Pensacola became the Southern League champion for 2025 with a 2-1 triumph to capture the series 4-3.

"Both clubs are talented. We just made fewer mistakes than they did," winning manager Nelson Prada said to the press corps at the postgame press conference. "We made the crucial plays and got the timely hits."

Knoxville skipper Lance Rymel agreed with that assessment, saying his club turned out to be "a little weak on fundamentals" at crucial times.

"I have a sneaking suspicion we will be working on fundamentals a lot next spring," he said.

On the way to the Round 2 championship, the Blue Wahoos ended up in second place in the South Division and compiled a 74-64 record during the regular season.
