Isea Grabs TL Reliever of the Year
Friday, October 17th , 2025
His manager and teammates call Edgar Isea a true bullpen ace.

The Texas League calls him the winner of the Reliever of the Year for 2025.

The Amarillo Sod Poodles relief specialist out of El Tigre, Venezuela, won the award with 28 saves in 39 relief appearances, 50 strikeouts in 39.1 innings and 25 walks.

He compiled a record of 2 wins and 3 losses and had a 2.52 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Evan Sisk of the Northwest Arkansas Naturals finished second in voting, while Cole Winn of the Frisco RoughRiders finished third.
