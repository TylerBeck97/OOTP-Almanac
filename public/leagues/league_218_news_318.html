Cook on Fire: Burns Toronto (FCL) with 3 HR
Thursday, May 8th , 2025
Miami (FCL) second baseman Zac Cook blasted three homers at TD Ballpark in the 21-3 Marlins victory over the Toronto (FCL) Blue Jays.

For the game, Zac Cook scored 6 times and knocked in 6 runs. When he hit his last home run, his teammates, hearing the crack of the bat, leaped off the bench and rushed on the field to greet the jubilant Cook when he reached home plate.

"It was just one game, but it sure was a lot of fun -- 5-for-5, three roundtrippers and a win," a gleeful Cook told BNN reporters in a postgame interview. "What a day."

Zac Cook hit a solo-shot off Bryan Borbon in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, was hit by a pitch in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Ismauri Bueno in the 5th, doubled in the 7th, hit a solo-shot off Danny Martinez in the 8th and walked in the 9th.
