Fans seated in the bleacher section at Frontier Field were taking cover all day long as Franchy Cordero zeroed in on them, rattling three long blasts off the seats in a display of Herculean power.

The big game from the Rochester right fielder helped the Red Wings take the victory, 10-8, over the St. Paul Saints.

"I told myself not to press on the last one," he told reporters in the postgame press conference. "I knew I couldn't force it -- it has to be a nice easy swing."
