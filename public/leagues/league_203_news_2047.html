3-Homer Hat Trick for Santander
Friday, September 5th , 2025
Whatever Anthony Santander ate for breakfast before his game against the New York Yankees, it gave him some impressive power.

The Toronto left fielder smacked three balls up and out of the park at Yankee Stadium. His home run barrage helped the Blue Jays defeat the Yankees, 5-4.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 3-for-4, scored 3 times, drove in 4 and put on a display that history will remember.

Anthony Santander hit a two-run home run off Max Fried in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Max Fried in the 3rd, struck out in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Matt Barnes in the 8th.

For the season, Santander has produced 152 hits in 502 at-bats for a .303 average with 43 home runs and gotten on base at a .351 clip.
