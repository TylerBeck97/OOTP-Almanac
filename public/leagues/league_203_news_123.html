Olson Hurls No-Hitter
Sunday, April 20th , 2025
Is it better to be lucky or good?

After leading the Detroit Tigers to victory, 6-0, and tossing a no-hitter over the Kansas City Royals, Reese Olson didn't have the answer.

"I don't know," Olson said. "To do what I did today, I'm pretty sure it takes both."

On his big day at Comerica Park, Olson fanned 10 and had 2 walks. And it seemed that when the Royals managed to hit the ball hard, it went right at a defender.

"That's how you get a no-hitter," Olson said with a smile. "A lot of skill mixed with a little luck."

Over the season Olson has thrown 32.1 innings and dished out 38 strikeouts with a 1-3 mark and a 2.51 ERA.
