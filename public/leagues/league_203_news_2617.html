Skenes of Pittsburgh Named Best Pitcher
Wednesday, November 12th , 2025
The National League announced today that Paul Skenes has been chosen the Cy Young Award winner for 2025. The Pittsburgh starter had everything working for him this past season as he soared to a 19-7 record.

In his exceptional season Skenes chalked up a 2.45 ERA in 209 innings with 261 strikeouts and 53 walks. Enemy teams hit .193 against him in his 33 starts.

He received 29 first place votes out of a possible 30. Kodai Senga of the New York Mets finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Jordan Hicks of the San Francisco Giants finished third.
