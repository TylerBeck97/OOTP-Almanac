Cardinals, Marlins Swap Players
Thursday, January 8th , 2026
Everybody is overjoyed with a trade consummated today by the St. Louis Cardinals and the Miami Marlins. In the deal St. Louis got 28-year old LHP Braxton Garrett from Miami and dealt 20-year old minor league RHP Leonel Sequera, 23-year old minor league LF Zach Levenson, 21-year old minor league C Sammy Hernandez, and 20-year old minor league RHP Xavier Cruz to the Marlins. The general managers that made the swap are happy and the players are happy, too. One of the GMs commented, "This was a good swap for all concerned."


Lifetime Garrett has a won-lost record of 16 victories and 19 losses with 320 strikeouts in 326.1 innings and a 4.03 ERA.
