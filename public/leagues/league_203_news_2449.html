Division Series Goes the Distance, Minnesota Prevails
Saturday, October 11th , 2025
In a series that had a little bit of everything, the Minnesota Twins have edged the New York Yankees.

The two rival clubs went the distance in the tightly-contested Division Series, with a 6-4 win in the clincher being the difference for Minnesota and giving them the ticket to the League Championship Series with a 3-2 series triumph.

"We never stopped believing in ourselves. When push came to shove we were going to get it done," said Ryan Jeffers, who was named series MVP.

For the series, Jeffers hit .353 with a .476 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Minnesota Twins will play against the Seattle Mariners in the next round. Seattle won their series against the Texas Rangers 3 games to 1. The League Championship Series is scheduled to begin on October 12th , 2025.
