Damon Keith Wallops 3 Homers for Comets
Saturday, June 21st , 2025
Oklahoma City Comets left fielder Damon Keith had a magnificent game against the Albuquerque Isotopes, rocketing three balls out of Chickasaw Bricktown Ballpark to help the Comets win, 14-12.

"I got some good pitches to hit and I creamed them," he said after the game. "The first was a big, fat fastball and the last two were hanging curves. All three were mistakes."

For the game, Keith went 4-for-5 with 6 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"There's a fine line between hitting home runs and line drives," Oklahoma City manager Chris Gutierrez said later. "Seems like Damon found his home run stroke today."

Damon Keith struck out in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Carson Palmquist in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Diego Castillo in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Victor Vodnik in the 8th.

This year, Keith is hitting .277 with 12 home runs and 27 RBIs in 45 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .389 with 24 walks and 26 runs scored.
