Lara and Gustave Traded
Thursday, October 30th , 2025
It's all done except the paperwork. The Boston Red Sox and the Athletics have agreed to a trade that will send 16-year old minor league RHP Ruben Lara and 20-year old minor league RHP Jhon Alcantara to Athletics, while 33-year old minor league RHP Jandel Gustave will be shipped to Boston. A top front office executive with the Red Sox described the trade talks as "quick and easy." "We're glad to get this deal done. We think it's something that helps our ball club and it didn't take too long at all to sort this one out," he said.


At 33 years of age, Gustave has put up these numbers in his career: 101 appearances, a 5-3 record in 118.1 innings, and 90 strikeouts. He has 1 save and a career 3.65 ERA.
