Erie's Graham Cops EL Best Manager Award
Friday, October 10th , 2025
When Andrew Graham was hired to lead the Erie SeaWolves, fans were expecting improvement over the last manager. Today, flanked by team officials, Graham accepted the Eastern League Manager of the Year Award, noting the level of passion for the Erie SeaWolves.

"The biggest challenge was the fans. I don't know how many times I was asked, if I thought the club was good enough. It was as if they expected failure. It was much easier to win over the ballplayers. Slowly but surely the fans began to believe in us."

Graham led Erie to a first place finish in the Southwest Division. The SeaWolves ended up with a 90-48 record.

Graham is looking forward to the offseason. "We're reviewing our options. You never stand still in baseball. You either get better or worse, 'cause everyone else is out to get your free agents or deal for your prospects. It's going to be a challenge." But the skipper has more than proven he is equal to the task.
