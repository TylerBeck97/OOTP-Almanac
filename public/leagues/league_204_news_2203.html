League Suspends Stripers McCabe 3 Games
Monday, September 15th , 2025
David McCabe of Gwinnett got more than he bargained for when he was ejected for arguing a third strike call in yesterday's game against the Jacksonville Jumbo Shrimp. It turns out that the ejection wasn't the end of it. Today, the league decided to suspend McCabe for 3 games for his actions which included a rant filled with inappropriate language and throwing equipment on the field once he returned to the dugout.

"Players need to show more restraint on the field," the league's announcement said. "McCabe obviously crossed the line and we had to take the proper action against him."

For the year, McCabe is hitting .218 with 7 home runs. His stats include 39 runs batted in and 27 runs scored.
