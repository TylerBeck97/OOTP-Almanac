Concepcion Selected DSL's Best Player
Saturday, September 27th , 2025
He may have less experience than most of his Dominican Rookie League peers, but that didn't slow Carlos Concepcion down at all in 2025.

At the age of 19, the San Francisco (DSL) right fielder has been named the winner of the Dominican Rookie League Most Valuable Player Award.

He had a .421 batting average and a .565 on-base percentage this year. In 56 games he piled up 90 hits, 13 doubles, 1 triple, 14 home runs, 55 RBIs and 73 runs scored.

"I'm blown away by the season I was able to put together," he said. "I'm looking forward to trying to do it all again next year."

He received 34 first place votes out of a possible 51. Pascual Archila of the Toronto (DSL) Blue Jays finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Pedro Reyes of the Philadelphia (DSL) Phillies Red finished third with 11 first place votes.
