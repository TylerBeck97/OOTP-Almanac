Ewing Will Sit Out 3 Games, Suspended by League
Monday, September 15th , 2025
The suspension of left fielder AJ Ewing did not sit well with the Brooklyn Cyclones. Both Ewing and his manager were livid at what both say is an unjustified 3-game suspension issued today by league officials. Ewing was suspended for "inappropriate conduct" during a game against the Wilmington Blue Rocks yesterday at MCU Park.

Ewing was ejected from the game for arguing a strike call. "It's not right," he said, adding, "I didn't do anything to deserve this. Eject me, but don't suspend me."

His manager also vented his displeasure. "Arguments go on all the time, face-to-face, and there's not a suspension. To be suspended is ridiculous. That's bull."

Ewing did more than just argue the call, he threw his bat at the feet of the umpire and walked off the field. That is the main reason the league suspended him.

So far this year, Ewing is batting .262 with 9 home runs and 44 RBIs.
