Hall Voted Best MWL Pitcher in September
Wednesday, October 1st , 2025
The Pitcher of the Month Award has been announced and the Midwest League winner is West Michigan hurler, Owen Hall. For his fine efforts in September he has been named the league's #1 pitcher.

During the month, Hall excelled with a 1.47 ERA and 3 wins and no losses. In 18.1 innings he struck out 29 and gave up 8 hits and 6 walks.

Up to now Hall has compiled an ERA of 4.76 with a 10-7 mark over 28 starts. He has 181 strikeouts in 145.2 innings and has held opponents to a .229 batting average.
