Ramirez Is NL's Top Rookie
Monday, November 10th , 2025
Agustin Ramirez started his National League career in style, capping it off with the Rookie of the Year Award for 2025.

The catcher for the Miami Marlins played in 126 games, hitting .251 with 112 hits. He totaled 20 home runs, 80 RBIs and 62 runs scored.

He received 30 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Dylan Crews of the Washington Nationals finished second in voting, while Gabriel Hughes of the Colorado Rockies finished third.
