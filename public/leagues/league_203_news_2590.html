Duran Named Top Reliever for AL
Saturday, November 8th , 2025
When Jhoan Duran jogged in from the bullpen this season, opposing teams knew they were going to have a tough time putting up runs.

The
Minnesota Twins closer turned in a dominant campaign and has been named the American League Reliever of the Year winner for 2025.

His award-winning season saw the bullpen ace get 43 saves in 63 relief appearances, 82 strikeouts in 65.1 innings and only 25 walks. He had a record of 3 wins and 4 losses to go with his 1.93 ERA.

"I hope I can pitch like this every year," he said.

He received 17 first place votes out of a possible 30. Mason Miller of the Athletics finished second in voting with 11 first place votes, while Griffin Jax of the Minnesota Twins finished third with 2 first place votes.
