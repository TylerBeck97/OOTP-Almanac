Rangers Trade Stephan to Braves for Ross Jr.
Wednesday, January 7th , 2026
It took some time to iron out the details, but the Rangers and Braves have agreed to a swap. Atlanta will get 24-year old minor league RHP Josh Stephan, 20-year old minor league 2B Erick Alvarez, and 19-year old minor league C Jhocsuanth Vargas with Texas receiving 36-year old LHP Robbie Ross Jr. in return. A source close to the trade talks said the two clubs were initially looking to make a bigger deal, but discussions broke off late last night. The two sides reconvened early today and eventually settled on this deal.


Ross Jr. has made 277 appearances in his career, putting up a 16-13 record. He has tossed 350 innings and struck out 287. Batters have hit .265 against him, his lifetime ERA stands at 3.91 and he has 6 saves.
