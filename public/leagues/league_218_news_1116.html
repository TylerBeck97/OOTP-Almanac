League Suspends Gallardo and Dougherty for Brawl
Wednesday, July 2nd , 2025
Moises Gallardo of the Washington (FCL) Nationals and Jack Dougherty of the Minnesota (FCL) Twins will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 9 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Nationals-Twins game at Hammond Stadium.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Gallardo and Dougherty will begin serving them soon.

Gallardo is hitting .125 so far this season with 5 RBIs and 8 runs scored. He has 5 hits including no home runs, 21 walks and .435 OBP.

Dougherty has no saves this season with a won-lost record of 0-1 and 6.23 ERA.
