El Paso-Las Vegas Fight Results in Suspensions
Friday, June 6th , 2025
Tirso Ornelas of the El Paso Chihuahuas and J.T. Ginn of the Las Vegas Aviators will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 9 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Chihuahuas-Aviators game at Southwest University park.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Ornelas and Ginn will begin serving them soon.

For the season, Ornelas has produced 66 hits in 236 at-bats for a .280 average with 8 home runs and gotten on base at a .342 clip.

To date this season, Ginn sports a 2-5 mark with a 6.68 ERA in 13 starting assignments. He has held the opposition to a .342 batting average and has 40 strikeouts in 63.1 innings.
