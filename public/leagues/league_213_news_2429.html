CAR Gives #1 Pitcher Award
Tuesday, October 7th , 2025
Nick Pinto of the Kannapolis Cannon Ballers had a tremendous season and was chosen the Outstanding Pitcher Award winner in the Carolina League.

He crafted a standout 8-4 mark in 16 starts, striking out 139, while walking 50. He worked 96.1 innings, yielded 86 hits and posted a 2.90 ERA. Opposing clubs had a .236 average against him.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 12. Riley Frey of the Augusta GreenJackets finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Ty Cummings of the Charleston RiverDogs finished third.
