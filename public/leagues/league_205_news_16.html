Monster 3-HR Day for Rushing
Sunday, March 30th , 2025
Dalton Rushing added his name in the record books after an explosive home run spree against the Tacoma Rainiers. But despite joining the select list of sluggers to hit three home runs in a game, it didn't guarantee a victory. The Oklahoma City Comets fell to the Rainiers, 9-8.

In his big day at Cheney Stadium, the Oklahoma City catcher went 3-for-4 with 4 RBIs and scored 3 times.

"It was just one of those days," he told BNN sportswriters in the postgame interview. "I don't know how to explain it. I'm just putting a good swing on the ball. "You would think three homers would win most games. It was good to hit 'em, but a win would have really made my day."

Dalton Rushing hit a two-run home run off Emerson Hancock in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Emerson Hancock in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Jimmy Kingsbury in the 5th and flied out in the 7th.
