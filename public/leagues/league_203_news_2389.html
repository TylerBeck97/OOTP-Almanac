St. Louis Takes Wildcard Series
Thursday, October 2nd , 2025
The St. Louis Cardinals and New York Mets hooked up in a classic Wildcard Series that went right down to the wire.

In the end, it's St. Louis heading off to the Division Series, after a 9-8 win at Busch Stadium tonight earned the 2-1 series win.

Outside Busch Stadium, whether they were supporters of St. Louis or New York, many fans talked about how it was a series they would never forget.

Jordan Walker played the hero for the Cardinals in the series, as his MVP performance included a .333 batting average, with a .429 on-base percentage. He also had 1 home runs, 1 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The St. Louis Cardinals will play against the Philadelphia Phillies in the next round. Philadelphia had a bye in the Wildcard Series. The Division Series is scheduled to begin on October 3rd , 2025.
