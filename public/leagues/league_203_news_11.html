Los Angeles's Garcia to Retire

Luis Garcia said at age 38, it's time to walk away from baseball.

At the end of the season, the Los Angeles Dodgers reliever has announced he will retire.

"It's the right decision for my family and I at this point," he said.

In his 546-game career Garcia has put up a 4.15 ERA and fanned 499 hitters in 527.1 innings. His career record stands at 26-28 with 15 saves.
