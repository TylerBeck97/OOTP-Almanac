Stephens Laces 3 HR Against Columbus
Wednesday, August 13th , 2025
Rocket City Trash Pandas first baseman Landon Stephens sensed he was going to have a good day at the plate, but he still might have underestimated it a little.

Prior to the start of today's contest with the Columbus Clingstones, Stephens told teammates he was sure he would hit a home run. He did -- three times.

"During batting practice I really had my swing down pat," he recalled. "I felt some thunder in the lumber."

The big day from Stephens pushed the Trash Pandas past the Clingstones, 8-4. To go along with the homers, he collected 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"I was seeing good, feeling good and swinging good," Stephens told reporters after the game.

Landon Stephens hit a sac fly in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Owen Murphy in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off David Leal in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Royber Salinas in the 8th.

This year, Stephens has hit at a .328 clip with 11 RBIs, 4 home runs and 10 runs scored.
