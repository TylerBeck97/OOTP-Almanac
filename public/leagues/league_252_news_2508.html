King of the Hill Announced by Florida State League
Monday, October 20th , 2025
The Florida State League made the official announcement today. The 2025 Florida State League Pitcher of the Year Award has gone to Michael Ross of Fort Myers.

He had a superlative season, chalking up a 8-2 won-lost mark with an impressive 2.83 ERA.

Ross logged 16 starts, threw 92.1 innings, yielded 73 hits, struck out 102 and held the opposition to a .215 batting average.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Jaden Hamm of the Lakeland Flying Tigers finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Ixan Henderson of the Palm Beach Cardinals finished third with 1 first place vote.
