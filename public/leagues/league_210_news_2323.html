Tourists Prevail, as Round 1 Goes to the Wire
Saturday, September 27th , 2025
Asheville Tourists won a nail-biting series against the Winston-Salem Dash 4-3, wrapping up the Round 1 finale 5-0 at McCormick Field.

Tourists first baseman Ike Irish was named the series MVP.

He batted .280, drove in 6 runs and scored 7 to lead Asheville into the next round of play.

"Ike was real solid for us, real solid," said Tourists manager Nate Shaver. "I normally say I'd like to have nine of him out there, but thankfully we got by with just one this time."

The Asheville Tourists will play against the Jersey Shore BlueClaws in the next round. Jersey Shore won their series against the Brooklyn Cyclones 4 games to 2. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes..
