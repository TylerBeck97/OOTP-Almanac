Lemos Smashes 3 Homers for Texas (DSL)
Monday, July 7th , 2025
Kleimer Lemos was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 12-8 Texas (DSL) Rangers Blue triumph at Baseball City Complex.

The 20-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Rangers Blue past the Chicago A (DSL) White Sox.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Lemos told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The first baseman went 5-for-6, rung up 7 RBIs and scored 4 runs on his record-setting day.

Kleimer Lemos singled in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Juan Felix in the 3rd, grounded out in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit a three-run home run off Cesar Bolivar in the 8th and hit a three-run home run off Cesar Bolivar in the 9th.

A look at his current stats shows Lemos with a .489 average, 5 home runs, 16 RBIs and 13 runs scored. He has played in 11 games.
