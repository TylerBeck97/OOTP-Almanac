Round 2 to Durham
Saturday, October 4th , 2025
It was a hard-fought battle for both teams to make it this far. But as the saying goes, "to the victors go the spoils." For the victorious Durham Bulls, that meant the International League Round 2 trophy.

The Bulls took a 10-5 victory over the Lehigh Valley IronPigs at Coca-Cola Park, winning their 1st championship after taking the series 4-1.

"We've got grit," said Bulls first baseman Tre' Morgan, who was named MVP of the series. "We know how to win when we have to."

The new titleholders completed the regular season with a second-place finish in the East Division and a 91-59 record.
