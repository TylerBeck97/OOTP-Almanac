Schneider Goes on Rampage Against Brewers
Friday, August 29th , 2025
5 times is the charm -- at least for Davis Schneider.

The Toronto Blue Jays second baseman tore up the Milwaukee Brewers to the tune of 5 hits in 5 at-bats at Rogers Centre. The offensive onslaught from Schneider powered the 10-0 Blue Jays win.

"Schneider was a tough out," Milwaukee manager Pat Murphy said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Davis Schneider tripled in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 6th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.

For the season, Schneider is hitting .211, batted in 25 runs with 7 home runs and 32 runs scored.
