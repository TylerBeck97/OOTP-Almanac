Another Setback for Atlanta's Joe Jimenez
Thursday, September 11th , 2025
"It's worse than we thought." That was the news from the Braves clubhouse today as it was learned that reliever Joe Jimenez will be out for an added 3 months as a result of a recent injury. His treatment for torn anterior cruciate ligament will have to be extended. Initially it didn't seem the injury was a major concern, but the Atlanta reliever told team trainers of some discomfort yesterday morning, and upon examination it was determined to be a more serious injury. "We'd rather take this thing slowly and make sure Joe gets back onto the field healthy," said the team's head trainer.
