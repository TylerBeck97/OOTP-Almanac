RoughRiders Win Round 1
Wednesday, October 1st , 2025
The fans in Frisco are celebrating the RoughRiders victory over the Midland RockHounds in the Round 1.

They defeated the Midland RockHounds 11-10 in the finale played at Momentum Bank Ballpark to capture the series 3-1.

Malcolm Moore was chosen the series' Most Valuable Player. The RoughRiders catcher batted .333, drove in 7 runs and scored 4 runs in the series.

"The RockHounds are a good club and I don't mean any disrespect to their pitching staff, but things just felt easy this series," Moore said. "Everything at the plate seemed right."

The Frisco RoughRiders will play against the Springfield Cardinals in the next round. Springfield won their series against the Northwest Arkansas Naturals 3 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
