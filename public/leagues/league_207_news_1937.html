Murphy Learns More about Injury
Thursday, August 28th , 2025
Shane Murphy will have to wait a while longer. The Birmingham starting pitcher was informed this afternoon that he will miss another 6-7 months with a ruptured disc. The injury has kept him off the field since 08/26/2025. Though Murphy had hoped to return soon, he has no choice but to be patient. "Gotta stay focused," he said. "Gotta stay positive."

On the season, Murphy has compiled a 4-1 mark with a 5.72 ERA in 8 starts. In pitching 39.1 innings he has 42 strikeouts and 12 walks while limiting the opposition to a .302 batting average.
