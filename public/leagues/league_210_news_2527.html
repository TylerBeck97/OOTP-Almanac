SAL Presents Top Pitcher Trophy
Tuesday, October 21st , 2025
The South Atlantic League announced today that Koyo Aoyagi has been chosen the Outstanding Pitcher Award winner for 2025. The Jersey Shore hurler had everything working for him as he cruised to an outstanding 9-1 record.

Aoyagi put up an impressive 2.34 ERA in his award-winning season. He started 14 times, worked 88.1 innings, yielded 90 hits, walked 34, fanned 125 and held opposing hitters to a .265 average.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Aaron Brown of the Asheville Tourists finished second in voting, while Andrew Landry of the Hudson Valley Renegades finished third.
