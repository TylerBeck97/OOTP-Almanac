Rece Hinds Drills 3 HR in One Game
Saturday, May 24th , 2025
The Toledo Mud Hens won't soon forget the display of raw power by Rece Hinds of the Louisville Bats. He victimized the Mud Hens by pounding three pitches out of Louisville Slugger Field to spark his club to a win, 12-9.

"I got three pitches down the pipe and popped them," Hinds told reporters. "Today I was in a groove. Tomorrow, I could just pop up the same pitches and go 0-for-3."

In addition to the homers, he collected 6 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

Rece Hinds hit a two-run home run off Troy Melton in the 1st, struck out in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Jake Miller in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Max Alba in the 6th and struck out in the 8th.

This year, Hinds has hit at a .366 pace and produced 31 RBIs, 12 home runs and scored 22 runs.
