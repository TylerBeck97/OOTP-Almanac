Findlay and Imanaga Dealt
Friday, November 28th , 2025
You can't always get what you want, but sometimes you find a deal anyway. The Baseball News Network is reporting that St. Louis and Chicago have made a swap with 23-year old minor league LHP Jack Findlay being shipped to the Cubs and in return the Cardinals would get 32-year old LHP Shota Imanaga.

St. Louis general manager Michael Girsch said the two sides were close to a blockbuster trade before agreeing upon a smaller scale transaction.

"This is just a bit of housekeeping compared to what we wanted to do," he said. "I won't name names, but the potential deal we were close to making would have certainly grabbed some headlines."

A source inside the St. Louis organization said the two sides might not be done dealing, and another transaction could be in the works.


During his career, Imanaga has 22 wins and 13 losses and a 3.42 ERA.
