Mazza and Moreta Swapped
Saturday, November 22nd , 2025
BNN has learned the Pittsburgh Pirates have reportedly worked out a deal with the Athletics. Sources close to the Pirates indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 29-year old RHP Dauri Moreta will be swapped to Pittsburgh with 23-year old minor league RHP Niko Mazza and 16-year old minor league RHP Rene Garza heading to Athletics. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.


To date Moreta's career stats show a 5-5 record with 2 saves and an ERA of 4.15. He has fanned 130 opposing hitters and held them to a .194 average.
