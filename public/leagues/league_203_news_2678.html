Miami, Texas Agree to Swap
Thursday, November 27th , 2025
After a week of negotiations, the Marlins and Rangers have come to terms on a deal that will send 30-year old RHP Sandy Alcantara and $3,200,000 in cash to Texas for 22-year old minor league C Malcolm Moore and 16-year old minor league RHP Alberto de Leon.

Miami will retain a portion of Alcantara's remaining contract.

In his career, Alcantara has compiled an ERA of 3.50 with 45 victories and 70 defeats and has held opponents to a .239 batting average. He also has struck out 936 batters in 1058 innings and given up 948 hits.
