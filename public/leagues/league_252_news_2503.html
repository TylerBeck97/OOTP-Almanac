Jupiter Manager Weisheipl Named Top Skipper in FSL
Sunday, October 19th , 2025
Manager Nick Weisheipl was speaking before a Jupiter fan luncheon when word came down that he'd been awarded the Florida State League Manager of the Year. Weisheipl's face turned to surprise as the club's owner made the hasty announcement.

"Wow... how lucky can a guy be? Manage a great bunch of guys... have great fans... have a great season... and now this... life don't get any better than this."

Jupiter fans applauded, recognizing how fortunate they were to have Weisheipl at the helm. Both fans and players in attendance toasted their skipper as they truly enjoyed the day.

2025 was an extra-special season for Weisheipl as he piloted the Jupiter Hammerheads to a 78-54 record and a first place spot in the East Division standings. A record good enough to win the 2025 Manager of the Year Award.
