5-for-5 Day for Bohrofen
Thursday, June 19th , 2025
Jace Bohrofen put on a hitting clinic as the New Hampshire Fisher Cats dumped the Reading Fightin Phils, 12-3, at Northeast Delta Dental Stadium.

The New Hampshire left fielder made Reading defenders earn their pay, lashing Reading pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Bohrofen said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Jace Bohrofen doubled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI double in the 6th and doubled in the 7th.

This season, Bohrofen has banged out 42 hits in 140 at-bats for a .300 average with 8 home runs. He is getting on base at a .409 pace and his totals also include 35 RBIs and 26 runs scored.
