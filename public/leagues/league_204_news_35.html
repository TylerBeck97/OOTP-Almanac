Isaac Connects for 3 Homers for Bulls
Saturday, April 5th , 2025
Xavier Isaac was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 13-6 Durham Bulls triumph at Durham Bulls Athletic Park.

The 21-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Bulls past the Jacksonville Jumbo Shrimp.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Isaac told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The left fielder went 3-for-5, rung up 7 RBIs and scored 3 runs on his record-setting day.

Xavier Isaac hit a two-run home run off Ofelky Peralta in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Ofelky Peralta in the 6th, grounded out in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Jesus Tinoco in the 8th.

For the year, Isaac is batting .324 with 11 hits and 5 home runs. He has scored 9 times and driven in 15.
