Best Pitcher Honor Goes to Arrighetti of Sugar Land
Friday, October 17th , 2025
It was a glorious year for Spencer Arrighetti of the Sugar Land Space Cowboys.

He finished the season with 15 victories and only 6 losses, with an impressive 3.22 ERA. The talented Space Cowboys pitcher started 30 games and struck out 202 batters in 176 innings and won the Pacific Coast League Outstanding Pitcher Award.

"It means a lot," Arrighetti said. "I hadn't really had a chance to sit down and think about it yet. It feels good right now. I can't really put it into words, but I'm sure later on tonight when I have a chance to sit down with my family and think about it, I'll have some words for it. But right now, I'm just happy that it happened this year."

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Braden Nett of the El Paso Chihuahuas finished second in voting, while Ryne Nelson of the Reno Aces finished third.
