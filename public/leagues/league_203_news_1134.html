Casas Whacks 3 Out of the Park for Boston
Friday, July 4th , 2025
Fans seated in the bleacher section at Nationals Park were taking cover all day long as Triston Casas zeroed in on them, rattling three long blasts off the seats in a display of Herculean power.

The big game from the Boston first baseman helped the Red Sox take the victory, 7-4, over the Washington Nationals.

"I told myself not to press on the last one," he told reporters in the postgame press conference. "I knew I couldn't force it -- it has to be a nice easy swing."

For the game, he collected 3 hits in 5 at-bats, drove in 4 and scored 3 runs.

Triston Casas grounded out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off MacKenzie Gore in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Erasmo Ramirez in the 8th and hit a solo-shot off Sam Thoresen in the 9th.

For the year, Casas is hitting .281 with 13 home runs. His stats include 40 RBI and 26 runs scored.
