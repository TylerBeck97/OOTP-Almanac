League Suspends Bogaerts and Rodriguez for Brawl
Friday, June 13th , 2025
Yesterday at Chase Field Xander Bogaerts and Eduardo Rodriguez got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the San Diego Padres and Arizona Diamondbacks. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Bogaerts will have to sit out 3 while Rodriguez would be given a 6-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

Currently Bogaerts has 9 home runs and a .262 batting average.

Up to now Rodriguez has compiled an ERA of 4.25 with a 6-5 mark over 14 starts. He has 79 strikeouts in 78.1 innings and has held opponents to a .229 batting average.
