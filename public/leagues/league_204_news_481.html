Caminero Sizzles -- Smokes Redbirds for 5 Hits
Friday, May 23rd , 2025
No matter what the Memphis Redbirds threw at him, Junior Caminero hit it. He easily handled Redbirds pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Bulls triumph, 9-6, at Durham Bulls Athletic Park.

"The way I was swinging, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Caminero's quip, Redbirds skipper Ben Johnson said he "would take him up on that next time."

Junior Caminero singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th and hit an RBI single in the 7th.

Batting .402 to date, Caminero has 10 home runs and 37 RBIs.
