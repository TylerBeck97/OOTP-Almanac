Santander Selected 2025 AL Most Valuable Player Award
Thursday, November 13th , 2025
Anthony Santander has been around Major League Baseball enough to know a good hot streak when he sees one -- and he got to see one firsthand all year. For his standout run through the 2025 season, he's taking home the Most Valuable Player Award in the American League.

The Toronto left fielder put up a batting average of .294 with 34 doubles, no triples, 47 home runs and 125 RBIs.

He received 15 first place votes out of a possible 30. Julio Rodriguez of the Seattle Mariners finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Lawrence Butler of the Athletics finished third with 1 first place vote.
