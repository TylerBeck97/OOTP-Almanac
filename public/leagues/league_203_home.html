Big Day with Bat: Perdomo 5-for-6
Saturday, March 28th , 2026
No matter what the Boston Red Sox threw at him, Geraldo Perdomo hit it. He easily handled Red Sox pitching, going 5 for 6 and helping the Reapers triumph, 11-0, at Fenway Park.

"The way I was swinging, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Perdomo's quip, Red Sox skipper David Beck said he "would take him up on that next time."

Geraldo Perdomo singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI double in the 8th and hit a GRAND SLAM off Aroldis Chapman in the 9th.
