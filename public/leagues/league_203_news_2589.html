Seattle-Kansas City Announce Deal
Friday, November 7th , 2025
BNN is reporting a rumored trade between the Seattle Mariners and Kansas City Royals has been finalized. The deal, which is expected to be announced sometime during the next few hours, will reportedly send 20-year old minor league SS Felnin Celesten, 22-year old minor league RHP Jurrangelo Cijntje, and 21-year old minor league LHP Juan Pinto to Kansas City in return for 28-year old LHP Kris Bubic. The clubs were noncommittal on the trade, but did not deny it.


Bubic has compiled a lifetime ERA of 4.27 with 25 career victories and 36 defeats. He has 540 career strikeouts in 564.2 innings and has held opponents to a .257 batting average.
