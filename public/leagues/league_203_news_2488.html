Mariners Dominate, Sweep League Championship Series
Friday, October 17th , 2025
The Minnesota Twins were simply overmatched by the Seattle Mariners in the League Championship Series.

The end result was a sweep, as Seattle has cruised to the World Series, with the final win coming at T-Mobile Park by a score of 3-2.

"We're clicking and running on all cylinders at the moment," said Julio Rodriguez, who was named MVP of the League Championship Series. "We're a dangerous team when we play like this."

For the series, Rodriguez hit .412 with a .474 on-base percentage. He had 3 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Seattle Mariners don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Los Angeles Dodgers - Philadelphia Phillies series. Los Angeles leads the series 3 games to 1. The World Series is scheduled to begin on October 23rd , 2025.
