Jenista Socks It to Giants, Cracks 5 Hits
Friday, June 13th , 2025
There was no storybook ending. Greyson Jenista of the Stockton Ports socked it to the visiting San Jose Giants with a terrific 5-hit game, but his team didn't get the win. The Ports fell, 9-7, at Banner Island Ballpark.

"I don't want to sound ungrateful, but something is missing here," Jenista complained following the loss. "When you have a great game you should win the game, too."

He definitely had an unforgettable day in the batter's box, ripping out 5 hits in 5 at-bats. Stockton manager Javier Godard sympathized with his first baseman, telling BNN reporters after the game that "Greyson played a whale of a game, but sometimes individual brilliance is not enough."

Greyson Jenista doubled in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Jose Bello in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Ian Villers in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

So far this year, Jenista has hit .465 with 10 home runs, 28 RBIs and 16 runs scored.
