Big Day for Barrios: Swats 5 Hits
Friday, June 27th , 2025
Jose Barrios was at the head of a Minnesota (DSL) hit parade at Minnesota Twins Academy. The second baseman went to the plate 6 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Twins to a nice 23-5 victory over the Boston (DSL) Red Sox Red.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Barrios told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Red Sox Red skipper Sandy Madera said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Jose Barrios grounded out in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and walked in the 8th.

This year, Barrios is hitting .300 with 21 hits, 1 home run, 9 RBIs and has scored 16 runs.
