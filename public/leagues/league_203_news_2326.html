Philadelphia Crowned Kings of the National League East Division
Sunday, September 28th , 2025
The owner of the Philadelphia Phillies called it a "proud day" for his franchise.

"Any time you win a division, it's a special accomplishment," said John Middleton. "I couldn't be happier to be associated with this group of players."

The Phillies have in fact wrapped up the National League East Division and reliever Orion Kerkering said the team is looking forward to the postseason.

"It's what you play for, the chance to win a World Series," he said. "Our guys know what's at stake and what this means for our team and city."

The club has a record of 92-69 and have secured the 14th division championship in franchise history. The Phillies will be looking for their 3rd Major League Baseball championship.
