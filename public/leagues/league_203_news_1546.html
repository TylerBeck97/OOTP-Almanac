Deals Estes to Kansas City for Marte
Wednesday, July 30th , 2025
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the Athletics and Kansas City Royals finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends Athletics's 23-year old minor league RHP Joey Estes and 21-year old minor league RHP Dairon De Jesus to Kansas City in a swap for 30-year old RHP Yunior Marte. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.


Estes has crafted a 7-10 won-lost record in his career with a 5.16 ERA.

Over the season, Marte has made 28 relief appearances with no saves. He has K'd 30 batters in 31.2 innings, yielded 15 walks and has a 7.11 ERA. Marte's record this year is 0-2.
