Jorge Touches All the Bases, Hits for Cycle
Thursday, July 24th , 2025
Requiring speed, power, and regular contact, the cycle is often seen as a test of a complete hitter. For Carlos Jorge, consider that test passed.

The 21-year-old right fielder hit for the cycle at Fifth Third Ballpark, to help the Louisville Bats triumph, 6-4, over the Toledo Mud Hens. All told, Jorge delivered 4 hits in 5 at-bats with 5 RBI and 1 run scored.

"It feels good of course, but I'm not going to kid anyone -- there's a lot of luck in hitting for the cycle," he said after the game. "Several unique things have to come together all in the same game."

Carlos Jorge grounded out in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Keider Montero in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and tripled in the 9th.

This year, Jorge has hit 8 home runs and posted a .263 batting average.
