Chourio Swats 3 HR Against Tampa Bay
Sunday, May 11th , 2025
Milwaukee Brewers left fielder Jackson Chourio sensed he was going to have a good day at the plate, but he still might have underestimated it a little.

Prior to the start of today's contest with the Tampa Bay Rays, Chourio told teammates he was sure he would hit a home run. He did -- three times.

"During batting practice I really had my swing down pat," he recalled. "I felt some thunder in the lumber."

The big day from Chourio pushed the Brewers past the Rays, 12-10. To go along with the homers, he collected 5 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

"I was seeing good, feeling good and swinging good," Chourio told reporters after the game.

Jackson Chourio hit a two-run home run off Drew Rasmussen in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, flied out in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Alex Faedo in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Ty Buttrey in the 8th.

This year, Chourio has hit 10 home runs and posted a .284 batting average.
