Great Week for Visalia's Jorge Lara
Monday, May 5th , 2025
The California League presented its Player of the Week Award to Visalia Rawhide center fielder Jorge Lara.

"His numbers speak for themselves," a club publicist said. "He's been really seeing the ball well."

Indeed, Lara has. Last week he went 11 for 23, batted .478 with 2 home runs and 6 RBIs.

For the season, Lara is currently batting .416 with 2 home runs and 13 RBIs. In 19 games he has reached base at a .505 rate and scored 21 runs to date.
