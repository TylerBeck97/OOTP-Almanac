Hewlett Named Top Reliever for NWL
Thursday, October 2nd , 2025
Stone Hewlett was a calming influence out of the Tri-City Dust Devils bullpen this season.

His teammates and manager said he had a poised demeanor about him that was typical of someone much older and more experienced. His poise has paid off, as the 23-year-old turned in a sparkling season and has been named the winner of the Northwest League Reliever of the Year for 2025.

The Leawood, Kansas native won the award with 5 saves in 41 relief appearances, 81 strikeouts in 63.1 innings and a record of 5-2 to go with his 2.13 ERA.

He received 6 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Kyle Amendt of the Hillsboro Hops finished second in voting, while Alberto Pacheco of the Spokane Indians finished third.
