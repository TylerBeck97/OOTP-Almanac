Hiura Pops 3 Out of the Park Against Guardians
Wednesday, July 30th , 2025
Keston Hiura went yard three times in a Colorado Rockies victory over the Cleveland Guardians at Progressive Field.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Colorado first baseman went 5-for-5 against Cleveland with 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the Rockies to the 15-9 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Keston Hiura hit a three-run home run off Joey Cantillo in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off John Means in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off John Means in the 6th and singled in the 9th.

This year, Hiura has hit at a .231 pace and produced 14 RBIs, 5 home runs and scored 6 runs.
