Milwaukee Deals Bitonti to Tampa Bay for Díaz
Saturday, November 29th , 2025
The Brewers and Rays confirmed this afternoon that a trade had been agreed upon. In the deal, Milwaukee will get 34-year old 1B Yandy Díaz and $140,000 in cash, while Tampa Bay will receive 20-year old minor league 3B Eric Bitonti and 18-year old minor league RHP Fausto Barreras.

Tampa Bay will retain a portion of Díaz's remaining contract.

All-time Díaz has played in 914 games, batted .281 with 85 home runs and 932 base hits.
