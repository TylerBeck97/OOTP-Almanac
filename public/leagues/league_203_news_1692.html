3-HR Game for Acuna Jr.
Wednesday, August 6th , 2025
Ronald Acuna Jr. went yard three times in a Atlanta Braves victory over the Milwaukee Brewers at Truist Park.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Atlanta right fielder went 4-for-5 against Milwaukee with 8 RBIs and 3 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the Braves to the 10-4 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Ronald Acuna Jr. hit a two-run home run off Jose Quintana in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, hit a GRAND SLAM off Jose Quintana in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Shane Drohan in the 8th.

Thus far this year, Acuna Jr. has hit .279 with 15 home runs and 36 RBIs.
