Solano, Gore Suspended for Fracas
Tuesday, May 27th , 2025
Donovan Solano of the Seattle Mariners and MacKenzie Gore of the Washington Nationals will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Mariners-Nationals game at T-Mobile Park.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Solano and Gore will begin serving them soon.

This season, Solano has hit at a .186 clip with 0 RBIs, 0 home runs and 4 runs scored.

This year, Gore has a 2-5 record and a 5.71 ERA.
