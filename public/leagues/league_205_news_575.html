Tawa on Fire, 5-for-6 Against Salt Lake

There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Tim Tawa is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-6 against the Salt Lake Bees at Greater Nevada Field. The monster effort from the 26-year-old right fielder helped the Reno Aces get the win, 18-3.

Tim Tawa singled in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, tripled in the 6th and flied out in the 8th.

Tawa on the season is hitting .320 with 6 home runs. His stats include 28 RBIs and 28 runs scored.
