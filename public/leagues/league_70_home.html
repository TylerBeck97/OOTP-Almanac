Crawford Selected AFL's Best Player
Friday, November 21st , 2025
2025 was a very good year for Scottsdale Scorpions center fielder Justin Crawford, this year's Arizona Fall League Most Valuable Player recipient.

Crawford had an impressive season, hitting .342 with a .408 on-base percentage. He totaled 38 hits, 11 doubles, 1 triple, no home runs, 13 RBIs and scored 16 runs.

He received 6 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Chandler Simpson of the Peoria Javelinas finished second in voting, while Jakob Marsee of the Mesa Solar Sox finished third.

Scorpions Top Desert Dogs
Wednesday, November 19th , 2025
It was an emotional clubhouse for Scottsdale after they claimed the 2025 Arizona Fall League crown with a 5-3 victory over Glendale in the final game.

Scottsdale starting pitcher and tournament MVP Jack Choate was too choked up to speak with reporters after the game.

"He played his heart out and it showed," said Scottsdale manager Buck Showalter. "He has wanted this his entire career, so it's a special moment for him and for the rest of the team."
