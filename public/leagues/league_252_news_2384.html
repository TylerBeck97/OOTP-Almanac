Bullpen Ace Wins Best FSL Pitcher
Wednesday, October 1st , 2025
Ramon Suarez had an excellent September and has been rewarded with the Florida State League Pitcher of the Month Award. The Dunedin relief ace was one tough customer on the mound, constantly coming in and getting his club out of trouble or protecting a one-run or two-run lead.

During the month, the Blue Jays star had 2 saves in 2 save opportunities, a 2.08 ERA and 2 wins and no losses. In 13 innings Suarez struck out 15 and gave up 6 hits and 4 walks.

So far this year, Suarez has delivered 17 saves. In 27 relief appearances he has 41 strikeouts in 31.1 innings with a 2.01 ERA and a 3-1 won-lost mark.
