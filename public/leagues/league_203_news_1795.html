Rangers Fermin Sizzles with Cycle
Friday, August 15th , 2025
Getting 4 hits in a game is no easy feat, but doing it to hit for the cycle is even more rare.

Freddy Fermin of the Texas Rangers pulled it off at Rogers Centre, going 4-for-5 against the Toronto Blue Jays. His batting escapades sparked the Rangers to a win, 10-6.

Fermin tallied 2 runs and drove in 4 during the game. Hitting for the cycle is particularly rare because of the different skills required - enough power for a home run, enough speed for a triple and enough patience at the plate to see at least four hittable balls.

"It just happened," Fermin said to reporters after the win. "Must have been fate or something, I wasn't expecting it. Heck, I wasn't even really trying for it."

Freddy Fermin singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Kevin Gausman in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, hit a two-run triple in the 8th and grounded out in the 9th.

So far this year, Fermin is hitting .269 with 9 home runs.
