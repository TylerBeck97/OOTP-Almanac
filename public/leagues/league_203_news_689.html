MLB News Flash: First-Year Draft Combine
Saturday, June 7th , 2025
The Major League Baseball Draft Combine kicks off today and runs for 5 days!
Excitement around this yearly event has built to a crescendo, and with the draft just around the corner all eyes will be on the combine for prospects to set themselves apart. On the field and in the training facilities, players will get to show off their skills in front of interested teams, scouts and coaches. Off the field they will be able to develop relationships with these organizations, helping drive draft day decisions.

73 of the top 100 pipeline prospects will be attending this year, notably:

#1 ranked draft prospect, 3B Ethan Holliday, 18 year-old HS Senior out of Stillwater (OK) High School
#5 ranked draft prospect, RHP Ben Blair, 20 year-old HS Senior out of Liberty University
#3 ranked draft prospect, RHP Tyler Bremner, 21 year-old CO Junior out of UC Santa Barbara
#4 ranked draft prospect, RHP Patrick Forbes, 20 year-old CO Junior out of University of Louisville
#7 ranked draft prospect, CF Sean Gamble, 18 year-old HS Senior out of IMG Academy Silver
#9 ranked draft prospect, SS Kayson Cunningham, 18 year-old HS Senior out of Johnson
