Worcester Wins Top Spot in East Division
Tuesday, September 16th , 2025
Members of the Worcester Red Sox proudly donned their East Division championship apparel tonight, after winning the division for the 1st time in franchise history.

"We don't take an accomplishment like this for granted. There are some good teams in our division, so it means a lot to come out on top," said starting pitcher David Sandlin. "We didn't know what sort of year it would take, but at 96-49, we're happy with the division title."

The Red Sox will be trying to bring the 1st International League championship to Worcester.
