CAL Announces Best Pitcher for August
Monday, September 1st , 2025
The pick for the California League's best pitcher in August is relief specialist, Kyle Roche. The Inland Empire pitcher compiled a great month to take home the award.

"I just go out there and try and get the job done," Roche told the press. "Things worked out real well last month and I appreciate the recognition."

The 23-year-old reliever threw 20 innings in 4 relief appearances as he piled up no saves, 20 strikeouts, and notched a 3-0 record and 2.70 ERA for the month.

This season, Roche has picked up no saves and posted a 4-0 record with a 3.97 ERA. He has 14 relief appearances, tossed 47.2 innings and struck out 53.
