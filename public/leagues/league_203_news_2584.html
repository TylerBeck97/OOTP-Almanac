San Diego-Arizona Deal Confirmed
Thursday, November 6th , 2025
The Baseball News Network is reporting that San Diego and Arizona have agreed in principle to a swap that will send 27-year old RHP Jhony Brito to the Diamondbacks with 19-year old minor league LF Slade Caldwell joining the Padres. San Diego general manager Tony Reagins would not confirm the trade, but a team representative close to the negotiations said the deal is official and is now only awaiting approval from the league.


During his career, the 27-year-old Brito has piled up 248 innings. Over that time, he has fanned 208 batters, walked 67 and put up a 3.59 ERA. His lifetime won-lost mark is 17-14 and opposing clubs have hit .247 against him.
