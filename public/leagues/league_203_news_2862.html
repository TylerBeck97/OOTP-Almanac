-Toronto Announce Deal
Wednesday, January 7th , 2026
The Athletics and Blue Jays confirmed this afternoon that a trade had been agreed upon. In the deal, Athletics will get 31-year old RHP Jose Berrios and $890,000 in cash, while Toronto will receive 28-year old CF JJ Bleday and 23-year old minor league RHP Nathan Dettmer.

Toronto will retain a portion of Berrios's remaining contract.

During his career, Bleday has compiled a .211 batting average and collected 330 hits, 53 home runs and 157 RBIs.

Berrios has authored a 114-87 won-lost record in his career with a 4.07 ERA.
