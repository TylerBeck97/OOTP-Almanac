Sanchez 5-for-7 for Shorebirds
Wednesday, May 7th , 2025
Delmarva Shorebirds left fielder Jordan Sanchez had a day to remember at Arthur W. Perdue Stadium, ripping apart the Fayetteville Woodpeckers and sparking the 27-9 Shorebirds win.

Sanchez wrote his name in the record books with a 5-hit barrage, finishing his special day 5-for-7.

"I'm usually a first ball hitter," Sanchez told BNN reporters in the postgame press conference. "I think pitchers think batters are going to let the first one go by. I never do. I've always found that it's better to pull the trigger early in the count. Pitchers get real nasty when they get ahead of you, so I try to avoid it."

Jordan Sanchez hit an RBI double in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, struck out in the 5th, grounded out in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Robier Hernandez in the 7th and doubled in the 8th.
