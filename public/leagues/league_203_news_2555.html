A.J. Preller Fired as San Diego GM
Thursday, October 30th , 2025
In a move that surprised few, the San Diego Padres have dismissed general manager A.J. Preller.

Preller's job security has been in doubt for some time, as the Padres have lost games, whiffed on key draft picks and not been able to engineer a turnaround through free agency.

Preller seemed ready to move on, and even before today's announcement few observers expected him to stick around into next season.

"I will cherish my time in San Diego," Preller said. "I'm sorry it had to end this way but it is clear the relationship was not working."
