Big Day for Lile: Swats 5 Hits
Tuesday, April 22nd , 2025
Today at CHS Field, Daylen Lile put on an impressive display.

The Rochester Red Wings center fielder tagged St. Paul pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats, but his teammates were unable to capitalize on the spree and lost the game to the St. Paul Saints by the score of 14-6.

"The loss takes some of the joy out of it, but I'll take it," Lile said. "5-for-5 ain't bad in any league."

Despite the terrific performance, the Red Wings manager was unwilling to offer the center fielder much praise, saying the "whole roster" is held accountable for losses.

"Daylen told me 'Coach, I had 5 hits' and I said 'Well that's great, but it looks like we needed one more from you,'" manager Matt LeCroy said.

Daylen Lile singled in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.


This year, Lile is batting .275 with 2 home runs and 9 RBIs.
