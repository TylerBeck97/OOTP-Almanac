Cedeño Touches All Bases, Hits for Cycle
Thursday, August 7th , 2025
Oliver Cedeño had a special day at Baseball City Complex, contributing to the 20-3 Texas (DSL) Rangers Blue win and hitting for the cycle along the way.

So far this season, Cedeño has put up a .383 batting average with 41 hits, 1 home run and 20 RBIs.
