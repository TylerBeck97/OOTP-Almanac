Alexander Raps Albuquerque for 5 Hits
Sunday, August 3rd , 2025
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-6 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for CJ Alexander, it came around today.

The 29-year-old third baseman scattered hits all over Las Vegas Ballpark to pace the 9-8 Las Vegas Aviators victory over the Albuquerque Isotopes.

Albuquerque skipper Pedro Lopez told reporters in the press room that "Alexander deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Alexander hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

CJ Alexander hit a solo-shot off Brody Brecht in the 1st, tripled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 6th, grounded out in the 8th and doubled in the 10th.

In his season totals, Alexander has 30 home runs, .297 BA, .370 OBP, 72 RBIs and 75 runs scored. He has played in 91 games.
