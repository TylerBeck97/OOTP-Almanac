League Suspends Moya and Carela for Brawl
Saturday, August 2nd , 2025
If there wasn't a history between Jonathan Moya of Detroit (DSL) and Enmanuel Carela of Washington (DSL), then there is one now. The incident between the two sparked a bench-clearing scrum with those two being the center of attention. Now they will miss 3 and 6 games respectively as a result of their actions. The league office announced their suspensions today.

"We're both really competitive," Moya said later, "and sometimes things go a little too far because of it."

So far on the season, Moya is hitting .316 with 3 home runs, 27 RBIs and 22 runs scored. He has 42 hits and played in 40 games.

To this point in the season Carela has 1 win and 3 losses and a 6.55 ERA.
