Delmarva Triumphs in Round 1
Tuesday, September 23rd , 2025
It's party time in Delmarva. The confetti is flying and residents are smiling.

The Delmarva Shorebirds have reached the mountaintop, capturing the 2025 Round 1 today. They defeated the Augusta GreenJackets 8-4 at SRP Park to officially wrap up the series 4-2.

Asked how it feels to be a champion, center fielder Alex McCoy was to the point.

"It feels great, like a million bucks. I'm so happy right now, not just for me but for all the guys," he said. "We put a lot of work into this and deserve to be champions."

The team is planning a victory parade and fan appreciation day later in the week. Delmarva finished the regular season 72-60 and claimed first place in the North.
