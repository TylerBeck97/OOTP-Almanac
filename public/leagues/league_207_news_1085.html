Trash Pandas Starter Named June's Premier Pitcher
Tuesday, July 1st , 2025
The Southern League Pitcher of the Month honor has been given to A.J. Block of Rocket City for a superlative month.

"I actually never wanted to be a pitcher," Block told reporters when asked who he credits for his pitching ability. "I was an outfielder until high school and on the third day of tryouts my coach came up and said, 'Son you couldn't hit a lick even if we put it on a tee for ya.' Well, that ticked me off, so I decided if I couldn't hit I wanted to make sure no one else could either and so here I am."

It all turned out for the best as the Rocket City starter notched a 3-0 record with a 0.67 ERA last month. He made 4 starts and struck out 35 in 26.2 innings.

This season, Block has gone 4-0 with an ERA of 0.55, posting 47 strikeouts in 33 innings.
