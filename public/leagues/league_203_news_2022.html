Butler Rips 3 Homers Against Cardinals
Monday, September 1st , 2025
Lawrence Butler brutalized the St. Louis Cardinals pitching staff, launching three home runs in the 9-1 Athletics victory at Busch Stadium. The Athletics right fielder was red-hot, the ball just exploding off his bat all day.

"I don't try to hit homers," he said later. "I try to go out there and put a good swing on the ball, and sometimes it is going to go out of the park. I didn't try to hit the first one or the second one or the third one."

For the game, he went 3-for-4 with 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Too much Lawrence Butler today," Cardinals manager Oliver Marmol said. "That's my assessment of the game. That's what our problems boiled down to."

Lawrence Butler flied out in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Erick Fedde in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Ryan Fernandez in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Hector Neris in the 8th.

This season, Butler is swinging the bat at a .267 pace. He has hit 34 home runs and scored 83 times, while driving in 81. His on-base percentage is .323 and he has played in 127 games.
