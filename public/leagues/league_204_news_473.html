Gilbert Belts 3 HR Against Scranton WB
Wednesday, May 21st , 2025
Fans are still buzzing after Syracuse whipped the Scranton WB RailRiders by the score of 18-2 at PNC Field.

Drew Gilbert smashed three home runs in the contest, a feat that would be a career highlight for most players and few fans ever see. In a tremendous performance, the right fielder bashed out 4 hits in 5 at-bats, had 7 RBIs and scored 5 times.

At the postgame press conference, Gilbert said it was an "unbelievable" feeling to hit three home runs in a game.

"Getting one homer is special, and two is great," Gilbert said. "Three is just... wow."

Drew Gilbert hit a solo-shot off Elmer Rodriguez-Cruz in the 2nd, grounded out in the 3rd, walked in a run in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Clayton Beeter in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Franklin German in the 8th.

In his season totals, Gilbert has 4 home runs, .287 BA, .411 OBP, 21 RBIs and 16 runs scored. He has played in 25 games.
