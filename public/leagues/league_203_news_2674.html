San Francisco, Cincinnati Confirm Trade
Tuesday, November 25th , 2025
Signed ... sealed ... and delivered.

San Francisco has worked out terms of a deal to send 34-year old LHP Robbie Ray and $2,340,000 in cash to Cincinnati for 19-year old minor league LF Arnaldo Lantigua. Both teams expect to introduce their new players at press conferences later in the week.

San Francisco will retain a portion of Ray's remaining contract.

Thus far in his career, Ray has worked 1408 innings with 1725 strikeouts en route to a 82-82 record. The 34-year-old also has a 3.98 ERA.
