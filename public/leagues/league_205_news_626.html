Pacific Coast League Announces #1 Player
Monday, June 2nd , 2025
Reno's Jorge Barrosa would not be denied the Pacific Coast League Player of the Week Award for this past week. His consistent stroke, which produced 11 hits in 25 at-bats and a .440 average, made him the clear favorite to win the honor.

"You just go out there and do what you have to do," said Barrosa, "When I'm up, I try to focus and block everything else out of my mind and have a good at-bat. I'm up there to get a hit or advance a runner, not to get caught up in the emotion of the moment. I hope I can win this award every week."

He also tallied 3 home runs, 9 RBIs, and scored 6 runs.

This year, Barrosa is hitting .316, produced 33 RBIs and 7 home runs, scored 46 times and gotten on base at a .421 rate.
