League Suspends Jenista and Chavez for Fisticuffs
Thursday, September 4th , 2025
After an intense confrontation, both the Woodpeckers Greyson Jenista and the RiverDogs Seth Chavez were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Fayetteville-Charleston game. Greyson Jenista will miss 4 games, while Seth Chavez was suspended for 8 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Greyson Jenista commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

In 25 games this season, Jenista has compiled a .348 batting average with 32 hits and 10 home runs. He has also scored 31 times and knocked in 25 runs.
