All season long Austin Riley gave opposing pitchers fits by hitting .296 as he collected 167 hits, 22 doubles, 3 triples, 35 home runs and 97 RBIs.

He received 23 first place votes out of a possible 30. Bryce Harper of the Philadelphia Phillies finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Matt Olson of the Atlanta Braves finished third.
