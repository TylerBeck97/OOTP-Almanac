Rodriguez Grabs IL Reliever of the Year
Wednesday, October 15th , 2025
2025 was a year to remember for Andy Rodriguez.

The Durham Bulls closer was the owner of a 2.08 ERA this season over 43.1 innings of work. He made 40 relief appearances and had 27 saves, 38 strikeouts, 17 walks and a record of 2 wins and 3 losses.


He's also now got some hardware, as the 23-year-old has been named the International League Reliever of the Year winner.

"It's something I can tell my grandchildren some day, pretty special," he said.



He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 20. Tanner Banks of the Lehigh Valley IronPigs finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Erubiel Armenta of the Lehigh Valley IronPigs finished third with 2 first place votes.
