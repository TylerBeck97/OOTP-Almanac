League Suspends Alexander and Phillips for Brawl
Monday, June 16th , 2025
Yesterday at Smith's Ballpark Blaze Alexander and Dylan Phillips got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Reno Aces and Salt Lake Bees. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Alexander will have to sit out 5 while Phillips would be given a 3-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

This season, Alexander is batting .352 with 8 home runs. He has scored 43 runs and driven in 44.

Phillips has 1 save this season with a won-lost record of 1-0 and 3.52 ERA.
