Hernández on Fire, 5-for-5 Against San Francisco
Friday, September 12th , 2025
No matter what the San Francisco Giants threw at him, Teoscar Hernández hit it. He easily handled Giants pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Dodgers triumph, 9-3, at Oracle Park.

"The way I was swinging, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Hernández's quip, Giants skipper Bob Melvin said he "would take him up on that next time."

Teoscar Hernández hit an RBI double in the 1st, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th and singled in the 9th.

Hernández is currently batting .292 with 32 home runs, 100 RBIs and 100 runs scored. He has 155 hits in 530 at-bats.
