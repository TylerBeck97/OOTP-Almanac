TL Announces Best Player Award
Tuesday, October 21st , 2025
The Texas League Most Valuable Player Award has been picked for 2025 and the winner is Wichita catcher Diego Cartaya.

He finished his outstanding season with a .310 batting average, 79 hits, 18 home runs, 57 RBIs, .397 OBP and 41 runs scored.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Alejo Lopez of the Midland RockHounds finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Cooper Hummel of the Corpus Christi Hooks finished third.
