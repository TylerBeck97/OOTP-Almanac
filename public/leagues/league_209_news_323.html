Hops Rojas Sizzles with Cycle
Friday, May 9th , 2025
Hillsboro center fielder Anderdson Rojas had a field day at Everett Memorial Stadium, battering Everett pitchers and hitting for the cycle.

Rojas sparked the Hops to a win, 12-11, over the AquaSox by going 4-for-5 with 4 runs batted in and scoring 2 runs.

"I was seeing the ball great today," Rojas said. "I'm glad it translated into results on the field."

Anderdson Rojas tripled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, walked in a run in the 4th, was hit by a pitch in the 6th, flied out in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Wyatt Lunsford-Shenkman in the 10th.

For the season, Rojas is hitting at a .333 clip with 3 home runs, 6 RBIs and has scored 8 runs. He has 12 hits, 5 walks and a .455 on-base percentage.
