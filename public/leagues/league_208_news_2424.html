Round 2 Sweep for Springfield
Monday, October 6th , 2025
Sometimes the winner is the one who got the lucky bounces and the lucky calls, and sometimes the winner is just the one who played better.

It was the latter case in 2025, as the Springfield Cardinals topped the Frisco RoughRiders 10-9 tonight at Hammons Field to win the Round 2. It was the 1st title in franchise history.

"When you play for championships, you have to play better than the team in the other dugout. We did that in this series, and all year. Now we're the best team in baseball," said Springfield second baseman Cesar Prieto, who was named series MVP.

The Cardinals took the series 3-0. The new titleholders completed the regular season with a 4th-place finish in the North Division and a 65-74 record.

Springfield manager Patrick Anderson grinned and nodded when asked to comment on Prieto's words.

"Cesar said it best -- we played better than the other team," the skipper said.

Hometown officials announced they were already planning a "grand celebration" for the winners in the days to come.
