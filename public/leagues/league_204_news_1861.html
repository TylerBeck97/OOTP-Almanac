Loperfido Suspended, Out 2 Games
Thursday, August 21st , 2025
The Buffalo Bisons were hoping for a more lenient ruling from the league office, but they didn't get it. In yesterday's game at Sahlen Field against the Omaha Storm Chasers, Bisons left fielder Joey Loperfido was ejected by the home plate umpire after he vociferously questioned a third-strike call.

The commissioner's office issued a 2-game suspension, saying the league would not tolerate a player behaving in that manner. The disgruntled Loperfido refused to leave the field and kicked dirt on the umpire's shoes. His manager tried to keep him away from the umpire and physically had to remove him to the dugout with the help of the two other coaches.

Reached for comment after getting the suspension notice, Loperfido said he was too disgusted to talk about it.

So far this season, Loperfido has put up a .232 batting average with 90 hits, 25 home runs and 75 RBIs.
