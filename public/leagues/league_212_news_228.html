On a Roll: Benua Hits for Cycle
Wednesday, April 30th , 2025
The Visalia Rawhide topped the Inland Empire 66ers by a score of 9-4 at Recreation Park, but all the talk was centered on first baseman Alexander Benua.

The Visalia star hit for the cycle in a show-stealing performance, powering his club to the win. Benua went 4-for-4, collecting 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run to go along with 4 runs batted in and 1 run scored.

"I felt comfortable at the plate in a way I haven't felt all season," Benua said after the game. "It was like they were throwing beach balls at me - the ball seemed that big. And it just floated in."

Alexander Benua singled in the 2nd, walked in a run in the 3rd, hit an RBI triple in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Kyle Roche in the 6th and doubled in the 8th.

Benua is batting .365 with 3 home runs and 19 RBIs this season.
