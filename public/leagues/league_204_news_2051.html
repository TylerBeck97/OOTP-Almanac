Thrice Is Nice: Mack Unloads 3 Homers
Friday, September 5th , 2025
Jacksonville Jumbo Shrimp catcher Joe Mack put on a dazzling display of power at Louisville Slugger Field against the Louisville Bats. In the 8-2 Jumbo Shrimp victory, Mack penned his name into the record books, slamming a trio of home runs in one of baseball's finest single-day efforts.

"He can hit them out anywhere, right, center, left, it doesn't matter," Louisville manager Pat Kelly said. "He's super strong and stays on the ball so well."

Along with his three home runs, the 22-year-old had 4 hits in 5 at-bats, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Life will always throw you curves, just keep fouling them off," Mack said after the game, when asked about his hitting philosophy. "The right pitch will come, and when it does, be prepared to run the bases. Or trot around them, like I did today."

Joe Mack struck out in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Aaron Wilkerson in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Aaron Wilkerson in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Connor Brogdon in the 9th.

So far this season, Mack is hitting .293 with 26 home runs, 79 RBIs and 70 runs scored. He has 129 hits in 441 at-bats and a .349 OBP.
