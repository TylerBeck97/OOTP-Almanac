Moniak Named 2025 PCL Most Valuable Player Award Winner
Saturday, October 18th , 2025
Mickey Moniak of the Albuquerque Isotopes had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 2025.

The 27-year-old left fielder put up a .322 average and a .375 on-base percentage, piling up 123 hits, 25 doubles, 6 triples, 41 home runs, 113 RBIs and 79 runs scored.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 10. Brice Matthews of the Sugar Land Space Cowboys finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Evan Carter of the Round Rock Express finished third.
