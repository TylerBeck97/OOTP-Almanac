5-for-7 Day for Triantos
Monday, April 7th , 2025
It was a sensational day at the plate for Iowa Cubs center fielder James Triantos, but a tough one for the team, as the Cubs dropped the contest, 9-8, to the St. Paul Saints.

Even with the loss, Triantos was pure misery for the St. Paul pitching staff. He left them shell-shocked after going 5-for-7 in an explosive display of hitting.

"It's always good to get a bunch of hits in a game, but you also want to win," said Triantos, who is the 22-year old center fielder .

"Triantos really hit the ball hard and when you do that, you've got a good chance to do some damage," Saints manager Toby Gardenhire told BNN reporters after the game. "I feel like we threw him some good pitches, but he just put some good swings on the ball."

James Triantos singled in the 2nd, flied out in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 7th, singled in the 8th, singled in the 10th, walked in the 12th and flied out in the 15th.

In this current campaign Triantos is hitting at a .405 clip with no home runs and 7 RBIs.
