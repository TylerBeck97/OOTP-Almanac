September's #1 Hurler Announced: Sanchez Wins Award
Wednesday, October 1st , 2025
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask Dikember Sanchez, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The Nashville Sounds closer, 21, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .229 average and walked away with the International League Pitcher of the Month award for September.

Sanchez posted a 3-0 mark and a 2.89 ERA in 9 relief appearances. In 9.1 innings, he had 6 saves, 13 strikeouts, and 5 walks.

To date this season, Sanchez has fashioned a 1.45 ERA and rung up 8 saves for his club. He has 22 strikeouts and 9 walks. Opponents have hit for a combined .214 average against him and Sanchez has a 5-0 won-lost mark.
