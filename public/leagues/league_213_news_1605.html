Carolina League Announces July's Top Pitcher
Friday, August 1st , 2025
The Fredericksburg Nationals really got a lot out of their bullpen in July and Robert Cranz definitely played the leading role.
Today the Carolina League recognized his spectacular contribution, naming him the Pitcher of the Month for July.

He made 10 relief appearances and compiled 6 saves in 14 innings with 20 strikeouts and a 3.21 ERA.

This year, Cranz has chalked up 25 relief appearances, worked 30.2 innings, struck out 37 and issued 9 walks. He has 12 saves with a 4.11 ERA and a 3-4 won-lost mark.
