Gunnar Henderson Bashes Three over the Wall
Wednesday, July 9th , 2025
The old superstition that "bad news comes in threes" certainly came true for the New York Mets.

Baltimore shortstop Gunnar Henderson tripled up on the home runs to propel the Orioles to a win, 10-2, at Oriole Park at Camden Yards.

"We had our chances to win the game, but we just didn't come through," New York manager Carlos Mendoza said. "Henderson hit the heck out of the ball."

In addition to the homers, Henderson collected 3 hits in 5 at-bats, 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"Easy swings get you home runs," he said after the game. "I kept telling myself not to press. They have to be nice and easy swings."

Gunnar Henderson hit a two-run home run off Frankie Montas in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Frankie Montas in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Jose Butto in the 7th and flied out in the 8th.

For the year, Henderson has hit .275 with 13 home runs, 40 RBIs and scored 50 runs.
