Villar Batters Las Vegas for 5 Hits
Sunday, June 29th , 2025
David Villar put on a hitting clinic as the Sacramento River Cats dumped the Las Vegas Aviators, 15-4, at Las Vegas Ballpark.

The Sacramento first baseman made Las Vegas defenders earn their pay, lashing Las Vegas pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Villar said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

David Villar doubled in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Will Johnston in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, hit a two-run double in the 6th and doubled in the 7th.
