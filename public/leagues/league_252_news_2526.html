Julio Zayas Takes MVP Trophy
Tuesday, October 21st , 2025
The Florida State League has made it official -- Julio Zayas is the winner of the Most Valuable Player Award.

In the 2025 season, the St. Lucie catcher stood out with a .322 batting average, 23 doubles, 2 triples, 11 home runs and 59 RBIs. Zayas also had 47 walks, posted a .417 on-base percentage and scored 49 times.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Yilber Herrera of the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels finished second in voting, while Sam Shaw of the Dunedin Blue Jays finished third.
