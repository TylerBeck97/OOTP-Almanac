McAdoo Put in Cooler, Hitless Against Indianapolis
Thursday, May 1st , 2025
The 22-game hitting streak of Buffalo Bisons first baseman Charles McAdoo came to an end at Victory Field. The Indianapolis Indians held him hitless in 3 at-bats and also sent the Bisons down to defeat, 10-6.

Later, McAdoo acknowledged to BNN reporters that the hit streak meant something to him.

"It was special," he said, "but I think other people make a bigger deal out of it than I do, or the players do. It was what we're all supposed to do; go out and hit the ball and try to score runs. It was a cool deal, but it's over now. Let's try to start a new one."

This season, McAdoo has hit at a .361 clip and produced 28 RBI, 5 home runs and 18 runs.
