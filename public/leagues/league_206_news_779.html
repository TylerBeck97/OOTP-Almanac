Portland-Reading Brawl, Encarnacion and Castillo Suspended
Saturday, June 14th , 2025
Freili Encarnacion of the Portland Sea Dogs and Max Castillo of the Reading Fightin Phils will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Sea Dogs-Fightin Phils game at FirstEnergy Stadium.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Encarnacion and Castillo will begin serving them soon.

Looking at his present stats, Encarnacion has compiled a .200 batting average in 7 games with a .304 on-base percentage, while hitting no home runs and driving in 0 runs.

This season, Castillo has fanned 23 in 24.1 innings and collected a 0-2 record with a 4.44 ERA.
