2025 CAL Outstanding Pitcher Award Announced
Wednesday, October 22nd , 2025
The California League Outstanding Pitcher Award winner has been chosen and the trophy goes to Modesto hurler Shaddon Peavyhouse for his outstanding season in 2025.

Peavyhouse was quite impressive with a 6-2 record and a 2.77 ERA. In 10 starts and 74.2 innings, he struck out 107 and gave up 62 hits and 18 walks. Teams hit .225 against him.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Daniel Nunez of the Visalia Rawhide finished second in voting, while Kannon Kemp of the Lake Elsinore Storm finished third.
