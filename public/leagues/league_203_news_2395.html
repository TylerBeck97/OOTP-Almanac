Mariners Prevail, as Wildcard Series Goes to the Wire
Friday, October 3rd , 2025
The joy and relief on the faces of the Seattle Mariners players was obvious as they celebrated their Wildcard Series win over the Boston Red Sox.

"In a series like this, the smallest thing can make the difference in how it turns out," said Seattle left fielder Randy Arozarena, shortly after the 6-0 win that clinched the series 2-1. "We're happy we did what it takes to get it done."

The team is now off to the Division Series.

Arozarena won series MVP honors on the strength of his .333 batting average during the series. He also had a .571 on-base percentage and 2 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Seattle Mariners will play against the Texas Rangers in the next round. Texas had a bye in the Wildcard Series. The Division Series is scheduled to begin on October 3rd , 2025.
