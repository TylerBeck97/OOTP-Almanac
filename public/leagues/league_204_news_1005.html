Columbus Snaps Liranzo Streak at 22
Sunday, June 29th , 2025
Toledo Mud Hens catcher Thayron Liranzo finally met his Waterloo.

A 22-game hitting spree is now history after Liranzo was unable to make anything happen in 4 at-bats today against the Columbus Clippers. Fortunately for Liranzo and the Mud Hens, the rest of his team had enough in their bats to win the game, 8-1.

"We're a true team here," he said. "For 22 games I've been able to contribute, but I always believed these guys would take care of things if I couldn't some day."

In 64 games this season, Liranzo is batting .343 with 84 hits and 23 home runs. He has scored 58 runs and driven in 71.
