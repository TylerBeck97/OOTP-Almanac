5-for-5 Game for Shade
Thursday, June 12th , 2025
Colby Shade put on a hitting clinic as the Beloit Sky Carp dumped the Lake County Captains, 11-1, at Harry C. Pohlman Field.

The Beloit left fielder made Lake County defenders earn their pay, lashing Lake County pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Shade said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Colby Shade singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Donovan Zsak in the 5th and doubled in the 7th.

For the season, Shade is hitting .452, batted in 4 runs with 1 home run and 4 runs scored.
