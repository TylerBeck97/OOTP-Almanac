Gongora Tosses No-Hitter Against Wilmington
Wednesday, April 16th , 2025
Aberdeen Ironbirds starter Sebastian Gongora came up big at Leidos Field at Ripken Stadium with an exceptional 4-0 win against the Wilmington Blue Rocks. Not only did he whip them, but Gongora held them hitless, too.

"My arm was a bit sore in warmups," confessed Gongora. "Sometimes you go out there and you're not feeling all that hot. You get into the game and all of a sudden in the seventh inning you look up and see the scoreboard and think to yourself, 'Oh, my goodness, I have a no-hitter!' You're so focused on what you're doing, it just happens."

In the course of the no-hitter, Gongora collected 9 strikeouts and surrendered 2 walks -- a day good enough to get his name in the history books.
