Great Season For Contreras, Wins IL Outstanding Pitcher Award
Saturday, October 18th , 2025
Roansy Contreras walked away with the top pitching honors in the International League this year. The Norfolk Tides starter was named the Outstanding Pitcher Award winner for 2025 earlier today.

This outstanding 25-year-old hurler fashioned a fine 11-9 won-lost record in 31 starts. He threw 174 innings, giving up 163 hits and 60 walks and holding opponents to a .245 average. Contreras struck out 147 and posted a 3.57 ERA.

He received 14 first place votes out of a possible 20. Andrew Painter of the Lehigh Valley IronPigs finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Cameron Weston of the Norfolk Tides finished third.
