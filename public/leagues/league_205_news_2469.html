Reliever of the Year Goes to Klein
Tuesday, October 14th , 2025
He may only be 25, but Will Klein of the Tacoma Rainiers has already established himself as one of the elite relievers in the Pacific Coast League.

The reliever was today named the PCL Reliever of the Year winner for 2025.

He won the award with a strong year that saw him put up 5 saves in 44 relief appearances and 53 strikeouts in 46.2 innings. He had a record of 4-3 to go with his 3.09 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Marc Church of the Round Rock Express finished second in voting, while Angel Chivilli of the Albuquerque Isotopes finished third.
