Hope on a Tear, Hits for Cycle
Tuesday, September 9th , 2025
Tulsa's Zyhir Hope accomplished what few others have done -- he hit for the cycle, collecting a single, double, triple and home run in the same game.

For the day, Hope finished 4-for-4 with 2 runs batted in and 3 runs scored and helped the Drillers knock off the Corpus Christi Hooks, 9-4, at Whataburger Field.

"I didn't even know I was about to hit for the cycle until after," Hope told reporters following the game. "One of the guys mentioned it when I got back to the dugout."

Zyhir Hope tripled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Tayler Scott in the 4th, singled in the 6th and walked in the 8th.

In 2025 Hope has hit at a .277 clip with 28 hits, 7 home runs, 12 RBIs and has scored 19 times.
