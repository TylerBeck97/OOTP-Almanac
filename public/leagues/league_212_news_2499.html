CAL Hands Out Reliever of the Year
Sunday, October 19th , 2025
Igor Gil came out of the bullpen and worked through tough situations all year, and for his performance he's been named the recipient of the California League Reliever of the Year for 2025.

The 24-year-old out of Maracay, Venezuela captured the award with 8 saves in 41 relief appearances, 55 strikeouts in 45.2 innings and a record of 1 win and 5 losses to go with his 7.49 ERA.

"I'm thankful, it's a great honor," he told Baseball News Network.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner.
