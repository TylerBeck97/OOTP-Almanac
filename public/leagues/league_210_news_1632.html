Rosa Pounds 3 HR Against Emperors
Sunday, August 3rd , 2025
Jeffry Rosa showed off his power as he launched three balls into the seats against the Rome Emperors and sparked the Cyclones to a win, 8-4.

"I got some good pitches to hit and turned on them real good," he told reporters in the postgame interview. "I have always liked to hit at MCU Park."

For the day, Rosa collected 3 hits in 3 at-bats, scored 3 runs and had 7 RBIs.

"He was instant offense today," Brooklyn skipper Gilbert Gomez said. "Jeffry really put on a show."

Jeffry Rosa hit a three-run home run off Derek Vartanian in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Derek Vartanian in the 3rd, walked in a run in the 4th, walked in the 6th and hit a two-run home run off Samuel Mejia in the 8th.

For the year, Rosa is batting .316 with 5 home runs.
