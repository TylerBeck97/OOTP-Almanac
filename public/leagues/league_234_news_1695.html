Reyes Rockets 3 HR Against San Diego (DSL)
Wednesday, August 6th , 2025
Cincinnati (DSL) right fielder Yensi Reyes blasted three homers at Baseball City Complex in the 8-7 Rojos victory over the San Diego (DSL) Padres Brown.

For the game, Yensi Reyes scored 3 times and knocked in 5 runs. When he hit his last home run, his teammates, hearing the crack of the bat, leaped off the bench and rushed on the field to greet the jubilant Reyes when he reached home plate.

"It was just one game, but it sure was a lot of fun -- 3-for-5, three roundtrippers and a win," a gleeful Reyes told BNN reporters in a postgame interview. "What a day."

Yensi Reyes hit a two-run home run off Jesus Castro in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Leo Salazar in the 5th, flied out in the 6th and hit a two-run home run off Jorge Olvera in the 9th.

For the season, Reyes is hitting at a .449 clip with 8 home runs, 22 RBIs and has scored 25 runs. He has 31 hits, 23 walks and a .594 on-base percentage.
