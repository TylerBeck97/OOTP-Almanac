RP David Bednar, St. Louis Agree to Terms
Sunday, January 18th , 2026
The St. Louis Cardinals have called a press conference later today to announce the signing of reliever David Bednar to a 1-year, $4,440,000 contract.

Both Bednar and the Cardinals are said to be very pleased to come to this agreement.

Since breaking into the league, Bednar has compiled a 17-27 mark, has pitched in 313 games and has been credited with 120 saves and a 3.47 ERA.
