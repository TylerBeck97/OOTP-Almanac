Orioles-Padres Trade: Akin for Ocopio
Friday, July 25th , 2025
The Orioles and Padres announced a deal that was completed today. Baltimore sent 30-year old LHP Keegan Akin to San Diego for 20-year old minor league C Yoiber Ocopio and 23-year old minor league CF Alex McCoy. Both teams appear happy with the outcome of the trade. The general managers told the Baseball News Network they felt the swap was beneficial for all parties involved.


This season, Akin has made 47 relief appearances, recorded 4 saves and compiled a 5-5 won-lost mark and a 3.84 ERA. In pitching 63.1 innings, he has totaled 81 strikeouts and 13 walks.
