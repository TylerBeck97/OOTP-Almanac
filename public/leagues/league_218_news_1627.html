FCL Announces Top Player Award
Sunday, August 3rd , 2025
At the age of 22, Jackson Castillo of the New York A (FCL) Yankees could very well have his best years ahead of him.

It's a startling thought when you consider the year he had in 2025 was good enough to win the center fielder the Florida Complex League Most Valuable Player Award.

"See the ball, hit the ball," he said. "That's my basic approach."

The top hitter put up a .508 batting average this year, as well as a .677 on-base percentage. He played 41 games and racked up 63 hits, 13 doubles, 5 triples, 10 home runs, 36 RBIs and 53 runs scored.

He received 14 first place votes out of a possible 15. Ian Petrutz of the St. Louis (FCL) Cardinals finished second in voting, while Cole Moore of the Miami (FCL) Marlins finished third.
