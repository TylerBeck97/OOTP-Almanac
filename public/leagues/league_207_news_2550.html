Mangum Tops SL Best Player List
Tuesday, October 28th , 2025
Jake Mangum of the Montgomery Biscuits proved that you can step out of the shadows and onto the stage if you've got the talent and the drive to hone your skills. Hard work, long hours and a tireless resolve paid off for him today in the form of the Southern League Most Valuable Player Award.

Mangum hit for a .353 average while collecting 98 hits, 21 doubles, 4 triples, 4 home runs, 40 RBIs and scoring 43 times.

He received 2 first place votes out of a possible 8. Chadwick Tromp of the Columbus Clingstones finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Pedro Ramirez of the Knoxville Smokies finished third with 2 first place votes.
