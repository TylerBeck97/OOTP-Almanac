Anderson Raps 3 HR Against Tourists
Tuesday, July 22nd , 2025
Fans are still buzzing after Aberdeen whipped the Asheville Tourists by the score of 8-7 at McCormick Field.

Ethan Anderson smashed three home runs in the contest, a feat that would be a career highlight for most players and few fans ever see. In a tremendous performance, the catcher bashed out 4 hits in 5 at-bats, had 4 RBIs and scored 3 times.

At the postgame press conference, Anderson said it was an "unbelievable" feeling to hit three home runs in a game.

"Getting one homer is special, and two is great," Anderson said. "Three is just... wow."

Ethan Anderson singled in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Anderson Brito in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Jose Guedez in the 6th, struck out in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Alejandro Torres in the 9th.

Anderson is batting .274 this season in 53 games with 12 home runs, 43 RBIs and 32 runs scored. He has 54 hits in 197 at-bats.
