Super Swinger -- Genth Slams 5 Hits
Sunday, June 22nd , 2025
State College shortstop Harry Genth added his name to the record books with a 5-hit spree at State College Stadium, but found his special moment spoiled when the Mahoning Valley Scrappers took a shootout win, 14-10, over the Spikes.

"I would have much preferred getting only a couple of hits and winning the game," he confessed to reporters afterward. "It's hard to go back into the clubhouse and celebrate a 5-hit game when you also lost."

Spikes manager Brian Butterfield called Genth's game "a once-in-a-lifetime moment."

"It's a shame the guy can't really enjoy it," the skipper said. "It's also a shame what happened with our pitching, but at least that we can try to fix."
