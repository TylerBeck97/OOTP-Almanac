McCarty and Gilliland Swapped
Thursday, December 11th , 2025
BNN has learned the Chicago White Sox have reportedly worked out a deal with the Toronto Blue Jays. Sources close to the White Sox indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 24-year old minor league C Hayden Gilliland and 22-year old minor league RHP Nolan Perry will be swapped to Chicago with 30-year old LHP Kirk McCarty heading to Toronto. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.


In his career, McCarty has 8 victories and 10 defeats and recorded an ERA of 5.48.
