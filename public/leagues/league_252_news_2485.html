Best FSL Reliever Named
Friday, October 17th , 2025
Tyler Stasiowski was dominant and cool under pressure this year, and his standout performance has earned the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels closer recognition as the 2025 Florida State League Reliever of the Year Award.

"I was just doing my job, and my job is to get people out," said the 24-year-old out of Sebastopol, California.

The award winner made 35 relief appearances this year and had 11 saves. He fanned 57 batters in 47.2 innings, with 29 walks. He pitched to a record of 3 wins and 3 losses to go with his 1.32 ERA.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 10. Grayson Thurman of the Dunedin Blue Jays finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Riskiel Tineo of the Jupiter Hammerheads finished third.
