Caminero Takes Best Bat Award in May
Sunday, June 1st , 2025
When Junior Caminero stepped up to the plate in May he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the International League Batter of the Month award.

The young Durham third baseman posted a .402 batting average with 14 home runs, 46 RBIs and scored 30 runs. Caminero also worked pitchers for 11 walks to post a .455 on-base percentage.

Caminero is hitting .403 this year with 15 home runs, 51 RBIs and 34 runs scored. He has 54 hits in 134 at-bats, 14 walks and a .454 on-base percentage. Caminero has played in 33 games.
