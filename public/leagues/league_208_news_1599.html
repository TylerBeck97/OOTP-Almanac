Crowell Snags TL Top Hurler Trophy
Friday, August 1st , 2025
Tulsa Drillers hurler Wyatt Crowell was rewarded today with the Texas League Pitcher of the Month honors. Crowell, 23, realizes that pitching isn't all about how hard you can throw, it's about accuracy and about picking yourself up even when you've had a run of bad luck.

"I'd like to say I don't give them anything to hit," said the Tulsa starter, "but that's not true. Every pitcher gives up a long ball now and then. Usually there's at least one mistake in each at-bat, but you've got to battle through your mistakes and build some positive momentum. The rest will fall into place."

Things did fall into place for Crowell. In July he took the ball for 27.2 innings while notching 36 strikeouts, a 2.60 ERA and a 4-0 record.
