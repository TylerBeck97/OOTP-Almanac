Laverde on Fire, 5-for-5 Against Lake Elsinore
Saturday, September 13th , 2025
Dario Laverde had an answer for everything the Lake Elsinore Storm threw at him at San Manuel Stadium. The Inland Empire catcher went 5-for-5 in the game to put his name in the record book and give the 66ers a win, 6-0.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Laverde said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Dario Laverde singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI triple in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Laverde is hitting at a .325 mark this season. His stats show 7 home runs, 25 RBIs and 27 runs scored. He has played 48 games for the 66ers.
