Riley Goes Deep 3 Times
Friday, August 1st , 2025
Austin Riley was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 8-2 Atlanta Braves triumph at Great American Ball Park.

The 28-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Braves past the Cincinnati Reds.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Riley told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The third baseman went 4-for-5, rung up 3 RBIs and scored 4 runs on his record-setting day.

Austin Riley hit a solo-shot off Brady Singer in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Brady Singer in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, flied out in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Wade Miley in the 9th.

This season, Riley has hit 26 home runs and posted a .297 batting average.
