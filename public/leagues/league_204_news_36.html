Familia Crushes 3 Homers Against Syracuse
Saturday, April 5th , 2025
Christopher Familia went yard three times in a Scranton WB RailRiders victory over the Syracuse Mets at PNC Field.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Scranton WB right fielder went 3-for-5 against Syracuse with 4 RBIs and 3 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the RailRiders to the 14-12 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Christopher Familia grounded out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Blade Tidwell in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Irving Cota in the 4th, flied out in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Dylan TeBrake in the 7th and was hit by a pitch in the 9th.

So far this season, Familia is hitting .400 with 4 home runs.
