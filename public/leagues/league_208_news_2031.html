Most fans have never seen a player hit for the cycle, and most players have never done it -- but it happened today at Momentum Bank Ballpark. Miguel Andújar of the Midland RockHounds accomplished the feat against Wichita Wind Surge.

Andújar went 4-for-5 with 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run in the 9-0 Midland victory.

"That was one for the grandkids, am I right?" Andújar said after the game.

Miguel Andújar tripled in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Angel Macuare in the 6th and singled in the 8th.
