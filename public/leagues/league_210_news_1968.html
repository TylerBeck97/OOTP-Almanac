SAL Presents Batter of Month Trophy to Guillemette
Monday, September 1st , 2025
Garret Guillemette feasted on South Atlantic League pitching in August and today he walked off with the Batter of the Month award.

The Asheville catcher rapped out a .444 batting average while collecting 20 hits, 7 home runs, 19 RBIs and 16 runs scored.

This year, Guillemette has hit at a .327 clip with 56 hits, 17 home runs, 41 RBIs and 46 runs scored.
